# Chibi/Q-Version Cute Style (`chibi`)

## Visual Characteristics
- Super-deformed proportions: large head, small body (2-3 head-to-body ratio)
- Oversized sparkling eyes with star/sparkle highlights
- Simplified facial features with maximum expressiveness
- Pastel backgrounds with kawaii aesthetic
- Sparkle, star, and heart effects around characters
- Rounded shapes and soft edges throughout

## Best Product Categories
- Snacks and candy
- Plush toys and stuffed animals
- Phone accessories and cases
- Small collectibles and blind box items
- IP merchandise and character goods
- Children's clothing and cute stationery

## Example Prompt Snippet
```
"Chibi Q-version style, super deformed proportions, large sparkling eyes, kawaii aesthetic, pastel colors, sparkle effects, cute simplified features, 2-3 head body ratio"
```

## When to Use
- Products that are inherently "cute" or kawaii
- Gift-oriented content and unboxing reveals
- Young female audiences aged 13-25
- Products where the discovery or unboxing moment is the hook
- Collectible and limited-edition product launches

## When to Avoid
- Serious or professional products
- High-ticket luxury items requiring authority
- Products where trust and credibility are primary concerns
- Content targeting mature or male-dominant audiences
- Products requiring realistic proportions to demonstrate features
