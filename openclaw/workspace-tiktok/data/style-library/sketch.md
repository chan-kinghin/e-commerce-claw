# Pencil Sketch Style (`sketch`)

## Visual Characteristics
- Monochromatic or limited color palette
- Visible pencil/graphite texture with cross-hatching for shadows
- Architectural precision for product rendering
- Clean but hand-drawn feel with visible construction lines
- Technical illustration influence
- Selective color accents on key product features

## Best Product Categories
- Tech gadgets and electronics
- Design tools and creative equipment
- Architecture-related products
- Minimalist accessories and watches
- Stationery and writing instruments
- Professional equipment and tools

## Example Prompt Snippet
```
"Pencil sketch illustration, graphite texture, cross-hatching shadows, architectural precision, monochromatic with selective color accent, hand-drawn aesthetic, technical illustration feel"
```

## When to Use
- Products that sell on engineering or design merit
- Audiences that appreciate craftsmanship and precision
- "How it works" explainer content
- Technical product breakdowns and feature highlights
- Products where inner workings or construction quality is a selling point

## When to Avoid
- Colorful lifestyle products
- Food and beverage (needs vibrant color)
- Beauty and skincare (needs warmth)
- Products requiring vibrant, lively visuals
- Content targeting very young audiences
