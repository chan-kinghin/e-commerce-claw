# Japanese Manga Comic Style (`manga_comic`)

## Visual Characteristics
- High contrast black and white with screen tones (halftone patterns)
- Speed lines for action and motion emphasis
- Dramatic panel layouts with dynamic compositions
- Intense facial expressions and exaggerated reactions
- Shonen/seinen influenced aesthetics
- Detailed backgrounds with environmental storytelling

## Best Product Categories
- Gaming peripherals and accessories
- Electronics and tech gadgets
- Sports equipment and activewear
- Energy drinks and supplements
- Streetwear and fashion
- Collectibles and action figures

## Example Prompt Snippet
```
"Japanese manga comic style, high contrast black and white, screen tones, speed lines, dramatic expressions, shonen manga aesthetic, detailed dynamic composition, panel layout feel"
```

## When to Use
- Products targeting young male audiences aged 16-30
- Action, energy, or competition themes
- Gaming and esports crossover content
- Products where intensity and excitement are selling points
- Before/after reveals with dramatic impact

## When to Avoid
- Calm, relaxing, or wellness products
- Content targeting elderly audiences
- Formal or professional contexts
- Products requiring color to showcase features
- Subtle or understated brand positioning
