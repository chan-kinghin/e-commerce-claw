# American Cartoon Style (`cartoon`)

## Visual Characteristics
- Bold outlines with bright saturated colors
- Exaggerated proportions and expressions
- Clean vector-like fills with flat color areas
- Dynamic poses and action lines
- Disney/Pixar-influenced character design
- High contrast with strong silhouettes

## Best Product Categories
- Pet products and accessories
- Children's toys and games
- Party supplies and decorations
- Fun food items and snacks
- Novelty gifts and gag items
- Family-oriented products

## Example Prompt Snippet
```
"American cartoon style, bright saturated colors, bold outlines, exaggerated expressions, Disney Pixar inspired, clean vector aesthetic, dynamic poses, fun energetic atmosphere"
```

## When to Use
- Products targeting families, children, or pet owners
- Content that benefits from a "fun" or humorous tone
- Humor-driven hooks and entertaining product reveals
- Products where personality and energy matter more than elegance
- Unboxing and reaction-style content

## When to Avoid
- Luxury or premium-positioned products
- Professional or B2B products
- Medical or health-related products requiring trust
- Minimalist or sophisticated brand aesthetics
- Products targeting elderly audiences
