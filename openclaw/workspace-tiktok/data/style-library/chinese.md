# Chinese Ink Wash Style (`chinese`)

## Visual Characteristics
- Traditional ink wash painting (shanshui) aesthetic
- Limited color palette: black, white, ink gray with selective red/gold accents
- Flowing brush strokes and mountain-water compositions
- Calligraphic elements integrated into scene design
- Misty, atmospheric depth with layered mountains and water
- Elegant simplicity with negative space

## Best Product Categories
- Tea and tea accessories
- Traditional Chinese products and cultural items
- Martial arts and wuxia-themed equipment
- Calligraphy and art supplies
- Silk, textile, and traditional clothing
- Herbal medicine and traditional remedies

## Example Prompt Snippet
```
"Chinese ink wash painting style, traditional shanshui composition, flowing brush strokes, limited palette with selective red accents, misty mountains, elegant simplicity"
```

## When to Use
- Products targeting Chinese diaspora or sinophile audiences
- Products with a cultural heritage angle
- Wuxia or martial arts themed content
- Premium tea, silk, or artisan craft products
- Content for Chinese festivals or cultural moments

## When to Avoid
- Modern tech products or electronics
- Western-style consumer goods
- Products requiring photorealistic detail or vibrant colors
- Casual or humorous content tone
- Products targeting audiences unfamiliar with Chinese aesthetics
