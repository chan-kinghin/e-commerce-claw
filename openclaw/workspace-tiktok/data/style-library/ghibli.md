# Studio Ghibli Style (`ghibli`)

## Visual Characteristics
- Rich watercolor-like textures with lush natural environments
- Warm earth tones dominate the palette
- Detailed cloud formations, grass movement, and wind effects
- Characters have rounded, approachable features
- Miyazaki-inspired landscape compositions with depth and atmosphere
- Visible brushstroke texture in backgrounds

## Best Product Categories
- Outdoor and camping gear
- Natural and organic products
- Children's items and toys
- Eco-friendly and sustainable products
- Garden tools and outdoor living

## Example Prompt Snippet
```
"Studio Ghibli inspired, lush green landscapes, watercolor textures, warm earth tones, detailed clouds and wind effects, gentle character design, Miyazaki aesthetic"
```

## When to Use
- Products connected to nature, sustainability, or nostalgia
- High viral potential due to widely recognized and beloved aesthetic
- Eco-conscious brand positioning
- Products targeting audiences who value environmental awareness
- Storytelling content with natural settings

## When to Avoid
- Urban or modern tech products
- Luxury goods with sleek/minimalist positioning
- Products requiring photorealistic detail
- Fast-paced or high-energy content
- Dark or edgy brand aesthetics
