# Japanese Healing Style (`japanese`)

## Visual Characteristics
- Soft pastel color palette with warm diffused lighting and gentle gradients
- Characters have large expressive eyes and clean line work
- Backgrounds are detailed but dreamy: sakura trees, cozy rooms, cafe interiors
- Studio CoMix Wave aesthetic influence
- Light bloom and soft glow effects on highlights

## Best Product Categories
- Skincare and beauty products
- Food and beverage
- Home decor and lifestyle accessories
- Stationery and desk accessories
- Self-care and wellness items

## Example Prompt Snippet
```
"Japanese healing anime style, soft pastel colors, warm lighting, gentle atmosphere, detailed background with cherry blossoms, clean linework, Studio CoMix Wave aesthetic"
```

## When to Use
- Products that sell on emotion and aesthetic appeal
- Anything positioned as "cozy" or "self-care" related
- Female-skewing audiences aged 18-35
- Products where the unboxing or first-impression moment matters
- Content targeting East Asian or anime-appreciating audiences

## When to Avoid
- Industrial or heavy-duty products
- Tech hardware and electronics
- Outdoor/rugged gear
- B2B or professional tools
- Products where technical specs matter more than feeling
