# Watercolor Style (`watercolor`)

## Visual Characteristics
- Transparent washes of color with visible bleeding edges
- Visible paper texture underneath the paint
- Soft, spontaneous color mixing and gradients
- Light and airy composition with white space
- Floral and botanical element integration
- Imperfect edges that feel hand-painted and organic

## Best Product Categories
- Art supplies and creative tools
- Handmade and artisan goods
- Wedding accessories and bridal items
- Floral products and botanical items
- Ceramics and pottery
- Home textiles and fashion accessories

## Example Prompt Snippet
```
"Watercolor hand-painted style, transparent color washes, visible paper texture, soft bleeding edges, floral botanical elements, light airy composition, artisan aesthetic"
```

## When to Use
- Products with artisan or handmade positioning
- Wedding, celebration, and event content
- Audiences who value craftsmanship and aesthetics over function
- Seasonal content (spring, summer especially)
- Products where the "story behind the maker" matters

## When to Avoid
- Mass-produced consumer goods
- Tech products and electronics
- Products requiring sharp detail or precision visuals
- Dark or edgy brand positioning
- Products where durability or toughness is the selling point
